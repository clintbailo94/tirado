How It Works

From words to execution in minutes.

No jargon. No code. No finance degree required. Just say what you want — our proprietary LLM pipeline handles the rest.

01

Describe Your Strategy in Plain English

Say it however makes sense to you. "Buy when it breaks above yesterday’s high and sell when it dips below." No fancy terms needed — if you can describe it, our AI can run it.

02

AI Plans, You Refine

Our AI doesn’t just read your strategy — it asks smart questions to nail down the details. Take profit target? Stop loss level? How much per trade? Which assets? It makes sure nothing is left vague before building your plan. And if you change your mind, just say so — the plan adapts instantly.

03

Autonomous Execution With Guardrails

When you're happy with the plan, hit approve. Tirado AI automatically links to your trading platform — MetaTrader 4/5, TradingView, cTrader, and more — using simplified one-click webhooks. No manual bridge setup, no config files. Once connected, your strategy runs 24/7, wrapped in automatic safety checks: max loss limits, position caps, and hard stop-losses that can't be turned off.

04

Monitor & Optimize

See how your strategies are doing in one clean, simple dashboard — no confusing charts or Wall Street jargon. Run up to 10 strategies at once, and get helpful nudges from your AI portfolio manager. It watches your back and gives you a heads up when something needs attention.

Safety by Design

Guardrails that cannot be bypassed.

Every autonomous strategy runs inside a hardened safety envelope. These aren't suggestions — they're enforced at the execution layer.

✓

Max Drawdown Limit

Hard stop when drawdown hits your defined threshold — positions are closed automatically.

✓

Position Size Caps

No single trade exceeds the percentage of portfolio you set. Override attempts are rejected at the broker level.

✓

Correlation Circuit Breakers

If multiple strategies concentrate risk in the same sector or asset, the system automatically reduces exposure.

✓

Earnings & Event Blackouts

Strategies pause automatically around earnings, FOMC, and other high-volatility events you choose.
